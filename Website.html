About the Science Fair

Welcome to the Annual Science Fair 2025, where young minds showcase their innovative projects and discoveries. This year's theme is "Sustainable Solutions for a Better Tomorrow."

Students from Years 1-4 will present their research in various scientific disciplines, including biology, chemistry, physics, environmental science, and engineering.

The fair will take place on November 15, 2025 in room 501. Join us for exploration, learning, and celebration of scientific achievement.
